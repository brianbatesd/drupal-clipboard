Welcome to Building and Permitting Services, a division of Community Development. Here you will find information on permit requirements, processes and timelines, fees, building codes and inspections, and utility connections.

The City Permit Center is open both online and in-person:

In-person service is available on Mondays and Wednesday;s with hours from 9 a.m. - 4 p.m. Closed 12:30 - 1:30 p.m.

*Starting next week, the Permit Center will expand in-person service to Fridays.

Virtual appointments with Permit Center Staff can be scheduled on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
