City-held Public Records

Request Records Online

Make a request by postal mail, fax or in-person:

  • General Records Request Form (PDF)
  • Business List Request Form (PDF)
  • City Council or Staff Emails Form (PDF)

City-held public records include:

  • City department or program email, paper and electronic files.
  • Committee/commission/board or staff email, paper and electronic files
  • Official City records such as ordinances, resolutions and staff reports,
  • City Council meeting minutes
  • Vancouver Municipal Code (VMC),
  • Other related documents that have been acted upon by the Vancouver City Council.

Police Records

Vancouver Police reports and others are available directly from the Police Records Division.

  • Go to the Vancouver Police Records Division
