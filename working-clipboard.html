Webform: Business License Fee Surcharge Refund Request Form The Vancouver City Council has extended the city’s business license fee (employee fee) surcharge refund program. Businesses affected by COVID-19 safety guidelines can request a refund of employee license fees. You may be eligible if you: • Applied for a new city business license between April 1, 2021 and December 31, 2022 • Renewed a city business license that expired between April 1, 2021 and December, 31 2022 To receive a refund, you must have an active Vancouver business license and operate a business in one of the following categories: • Restaurant, Tavern, Brewery, Winery, or Distillery • Fitness and Training Facility, including yoga studios • Movie Theater • Theater or Performing Arts Organization • Bowling Alley If your business meets these requirements, you can request a refund by completing and submitting the form below. You will need a copy of your Vancouver business license and access to your Washington State Department of Revenue account.
