Let's Show Vancouver Some Love!

City of Vancouver, Parks Foundation of Clark County and Vancouver's Downtown Association are partnering to plant summer flowers at Propstra Square and spruce up around the downtown core as well as Uptown Village.

Volunteers are asked to bring water bottles, face mask, gloves, kneeling pads, trowels or other hand tools for the cleanup. Volunteers should dress for the weather and wear long pants and closed-toe shoes.

Youth under 18 must have a parental consent completed via the online RSVP. Youth under 14 must be accompanied by an adult.

Interested in supporting the flower planting at Propstra Square? Purchase a brick from the Parks Foundation for a friend, family member, pet, or someone else you'd like to commemorate. Bricks make great birthday, anniversary/wedding, and Valentines Day presents!
