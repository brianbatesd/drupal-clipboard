The Vancouver City Council has extended the city’s business license fee (employee fee) surcharge refund program to benefit certain businesses affected by COVID-19 safety guidelines. You may be eligible for a refund of the employee fee(s) you paid for 1 or 2 license periods if you:

  • Applied for a new city business license between April 1, 2021 and December 31, 2022, and/or
  • Renewed a city business license that expired between April 1, 2021 and December, 31 2022

Your business must also fall into one of the following eligible categories:

  • Restaurant, Tavern, Brewery, Winery, or Distillery
  • Fitness and Training Facility, including yoga studios
  • Movie Theater
  • Theater or Performing Arts Organization
  • Bowling Alley

If your business meets these requirements, please complete this short online form. Note: You will need a copy of your current Washington State/Vancouver business license or access to your Washington State Department of Revenue account to complete the request.
