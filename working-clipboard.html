Water safety and swimming skills are key to staying safe and healthy at all ages. Whether you are just getting comfortable in the water, ready to start basic swimming or looking to increase your swimming endurance, swim lessons are a great place to start!

Swim Lessons

  • All lessons taught by Red-Cross certified Water Safety Instructors (WSI).
  • Each session consists of 30-minute lessons with a swim instructor providing instruction in the water.
  • The instructor will wear a face covering whenever possible and will maintain a safe physical distance.
  • Group swim lessons have a 1:4 instructor to swimmer ratio and participants will be spaced out in the pool area to provide plenty of room for physical distancing.
  • Face coverings are required in the community centers but participants may remove them while they are in the water.
  • A community center pass is not required to register for swim lessons.

Ages: Ages vary by lesson format.

  • Parent and Tot: Ages 6 months to 3 years
  • Private Lessons: Ages 3+
  • Pre Level 1, 2, 3: Ages 3-5
  • Level 1, 2, 3, 4: Ages 6-12
  • Level 5 and 6: Ages 6-14

Cost per Session: Cost varies by lesson type. Each session includes four 30-minute lessons. Click here to see if your address is within Vancouver city limits.

Location: Firstenburg Community Center Pool (700 NE 136th Ave.) and Marshall Community Center Pool (1009 E. McLoughlin Blvd.)
