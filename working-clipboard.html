City-held Public Records

Request Records Online

Make a request by postal mail, fax or in-person:

  • General Records Request Form (PDF)
  • Business List Request Form (PDF)
  • City Council or Staff Emails Form (PDF)

City-held public records include:

  • City department or program email, paper and electronic files.
  • Committee/commission/board or staff email, paper and electronic files
  • Official City records such as ordinances, resolutions and staff reports,
  • City Council meeting minutes
  • Vancouver Municipal Code (VMC),
  • Other related documents that have been acted upon by the Vancouver City Council.

Police Records

Vancouver Police reports and others are available directly from the Police Records Division.

  • Go to the Vancouver Police Records Division

Washington State Digital Archive

Electronic copies of many current and historic city, county and state records are available on-demand on the Washington State Digital Archive website.

Birth and Death Records

Birth and death records are not processed or held by the City.

  • Go to the Clark County Public Health Department website

Marriage Licenses

Marriage licenses and records are not processed or held by the City.

  • Go to the Clark County Auditor's Office marriage licensing website.

Court Records, including Divorce Records

Court records are not processed or held by the City.

  • Go to the Clark County Clerk's Office website.

Property Records

Property records are not processed or held by the City.

  • To find property records, go to the Clark County GIS website

9-1-1 Call Information

9-1-1 call information is not available from the City of Vancouver.

  • Go to the Clark Regional Emergency Services Agency website
