The Vancouver City Manager has issued 17 emergency orders related to the COVID-19 pandemic since March 2020.

Active Emergency Orders

No. 2020-01 (PDF) ─ Declaration of Civil Emergency

No. 2020-14, Section 4 (PDF) ─ Extension of Specified Temporary Employment Policies

No. 2020-17 (PDF) ─ Extends the City Manager’s Emergency Purchasing Authority and Emergency Budget Authority through the end of the declared emergency

Expired Emergency Orders

No. 2020-16 (PDF) ─ Extending the duration of Emergency Order 2020-09

No. 2020-15 (PDF) ─ Reopening playgrounds in City parks

No. 2020-14 (PDF) (Except for Section 4, which remains active) ─ Related to closures of community centers, parking enforcement, landlord sanitation measures, park shelter rentals and parklets

No. 2020-13 (PDF) ─ Extending the duration of specific sections of prior Emergency Orders

No. 2020-12 (PDF) ─ Permitting Community Gardens to reopen once appropriate spacing and sanitation steps had been evaluated and implemented

No. 2020-11 (PDF) ─ Extending the duration of Emergency Order Nos. 2020-02 through 2020-09; Suspending park shelter rentals; Closing community gardens, playgrounds, water features, Water Resource Education Center; Extending utility ratepayer assistance

No. 2020-10 (PDF) ─ Suspending daily financial deposits as permitted by RCW 43.09.240

No 2020-09 (PDF) ─ Regarding Landlord entries into residential rental property; requiring specified sanitation measures be taken by landlords when entering residential rental property

No 2020-08 (PDF) ─ Regarding foreclosures, evictions, and rental property inspections; Adding a defense from residential foreclosures on private mortgages; adding a defense from eviction for small businesses (with less than 50 employees); Requiring specific sanitation measures when conducing inspections of residential rental property

No 2020-07 (PDF) ─ Permitting of public farmers markets; Utility ratepayer assistance; Funding of H20 account; temporary suspension of the Business License Fee and Business Licensee Fee Surcharge

No 2020-06 (PDF) ─ Temporarily closing Vancouver Police Department buildings to public access and modifying Vancouver Police Department operations

No. 2020-05 (PDF) ─ Suspending collection of on-street parking fees; suspending specified parking enforcement activities; waiving parking citations after March 13, 2020

No. 2020-04 (PDF) ─ Regarding infectious disease control within City workforce; Implementing temporary telecommute policy; temporary expansion of employment leave policies; temporary amendment of City hiring practices

No. 2020-03 (PDF) ─ Closing specified City buildings; Amending City Council schedules; Postponing annual State of the City event; Providing authorization to City Manager to enter into contracts per VMC 3.05.030 and access City emergency reserve fund

No. 2020-02 (PDF) ─ Canceling City-permitted public events; Closing specified facilities; Suspending specific classes; implementing City employment policy 407
