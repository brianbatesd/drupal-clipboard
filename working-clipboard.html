The Vancouver Police Department is seeking applicants for Entry and Lateral Police Officers to fill current vacancies.

Entry Level

Entry Officers Receive a $10,000 Hiring Incentive!

Salary: $6,301 - $8,444 per month/Officer

  • $10,000 Hiring Incentive
  • Education Incentive pay
  • + 2.5% to Annual Salary For Associate Degree
  • + 5% to Annual Salary For Bachelor Degree
  • Shift differential pay
  • Holiday compensation is 2.4% of base salary

The Vancouver Police Department works with Public Safety Testing to conduct our entry level police officer testing. To get started in the process visit: https://www.publicsafetytesting.com/

Lateral

Lateral Officers Receive a $25,000 Hiring Incentive!

Salary: $6,301 - $8,444 per month/Officer

  • $10,000 Hiring Incentive
  • Education Incentive pay
  • + 2.5% to Annual Salary For Associate Degree
  • + 5% to Annual Salary For Bachelor Degree
  • Shift differential pay
  • Holiday compensation is 2.4% of base salary

To qualify as a Lateral Police Officer, and receive the $25,000 hiring incentive, the Lateral Police Candidate must be currently employed as a sworn law enforcement officer who has successfully completed their Field Training and Probationary status with their current department. The Lateral Police Applicant must also meet all other standards as set by the Criminal Justice Training Center to be qualified as a Lateral Police Applicant in the State of Washington.

To get started in the application process please visit: Lateral Officer Applications.
