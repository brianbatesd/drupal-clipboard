Thank you for your interest in doing business with the City of Vancouver. The City of Vancouver is utilizing the Municipal Research and Services (MRSC) Small Works Roster for projects $300,000 or less.

To be eligible to work on small works projects, you must register your company on the MRSC Rosters at www.mrscrosters.org. To remain eligible to be awarded small public works projects you must register ANNUALLY on the MRSC Rosters and select the City of Vancouver. Questions on the MRSC Rosters may be directed to MRSC at 206-625-1300 or via email at mrscrosters@mrscrosters.org.

We encourage you to register on the City's Procurement Portal. You will be able to create a login and password when you register, which allows you to access City posted projects.

Specific questions for the City of Vancouver Small Works program can be directed to Patrick Murphy at (360) 487-8427 or via email at patrick.murphy@cityofvancouver.us.
