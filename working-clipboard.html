Important Information about Our Application Process:

  • Carefully read the media advisory for the opening you are applying for.
    • City employees are not eligible to serve on city boards and commissions.
  • Commission meetings may require day or evening attendance. Your accepting appointment to a commission affirms your ability to attend meetings as scheduled.
  • Upon appointment, proof of identity and residency may be required.
  • Applications are considered active for one year. You will be notified of future openings for those Boards & Commissions which you apply or indicate interest.
  • Please note that pursuant to the state's Public Records Act (RCW 42.56), applications submitted for these appointments are public records and may be subject to public disclosure.
